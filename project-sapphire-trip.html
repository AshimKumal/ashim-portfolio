The Challenge

To compile raw trip footage into an exciting, fast-paced promotional video that conveys the thrill and ambiance of the Sapphire Trip experience.

The Approach

I focused on dynamic pacing, rhythmic cuts synchronized with the soundtrack, and color grading that emphasizes the vibrancy of the locations. The edit maintains a high level of energy throughout, designed specifically to engage viewers on social media platforms and encourage bookings and inquiries.
