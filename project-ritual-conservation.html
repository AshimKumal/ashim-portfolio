The Challenge

To weave together footage of traditional Hākusaphū death rituals alongside the modern scientific conservation efforts associated with related artifacts.

The Approach

The editing respectfully contrasts the atmospheric, solemn footage of the rituals with the sterile, precise environment of the conservation lab. Seamless transitions and thoughtful pacing produce a narrative that highlights both cultural heritage and scientific dedication.
