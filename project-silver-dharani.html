The Challenge

To craft a compelling story out of the extensive research and preservation efforts surrounding the rare Silver Dharani manuscript.

The Approach

A cinematic editing approach was adopted, combining slow, deliberate pans over the manuscript with insightful interviews. Sound design played a crucial role in creating a scholarly yet accessible atmosphere, ensuring the historical significance of the manuscript was clearly conveyed to the viewer.
