The Challenge

Creating a seamless data visualization tool for enterprise finance teams. The project required merging vast amounts of raw data into an intuitive, visually robust interface that could be heavily customized by end users while maintaining peak performance.

The Approach

By conducting thorough user research and mapping out wireframes in Figma, I structured the interface around clarity and dark-mode aesthetic principles. This reduces eye strain for analysts working long hours. Micro-interactions and cinematic transitions were prototyped to ensure a premium feel across all breakpoints.
