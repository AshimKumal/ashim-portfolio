The Challenge

Redefining the brand identity for ACIC (Annapurna City International College) to reflect its commitment to excellence in digital arts and animation. The design needed to be vibrant, creative, and forward-thinking to appeal to a younger, artistically inclined demographic.

The Approach

I focused on dynamic visual elements, incorporating bold color palettes and energetic layouts. This helped create a brand image that is both professional and creatively stimulating. The new design system extends across all academic materials and marketing touchpoints, reinforcing ACIC's status as a leading institution for aspiring creatives. The final product is a visually compelling and modern brand presence that effectively captures the essence of the college's artistic mission.
