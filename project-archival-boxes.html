The Challenge

To document and present the meticulous process of preserving archival boxes. The video needed to be informative, respectful of the historical materials, and visually engaging without taking focus away from the subject matter.

The Approach

I utilized precise pacing, subtle color correction, and clear, informative overlays to guide the viewer through the complexities of the preservation process. The final edit balances educational intent with cinematic storytelling techniques, highlighting the dedication required in historical conservation.
